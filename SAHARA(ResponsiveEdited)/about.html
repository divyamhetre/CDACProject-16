Sahara Donation Website bridges the gap between the Donor and Needy people. We are an organization aimed to provide meals and clothes to the needy people. People wishing to donate food and clothes will be able to donate with help of Sahara Donation Website and that will be collected by volunteer. The donated items will be safely reach to the intended needy person .Needy person can also ask for help by visiting our website and providing the details about their need. Our project objective is to enable easy interaction between donors and NGO.
Our misssion is to end Hunger across the Country,Now we are helping the poor people who required the food in some locations.In future we will help people across the country to fight against Hunger.let's Do it together,Your contribution will end someone's hunger.
Our vision is to help the poor so that they can eat 2 times a day to live a better life.
